The Best Movies According to Avani

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Coco

"Coco" is a visually stunning and emotionally resonant film that masterfully blends vibrant animation with a heartfelt story about family and tradition. Its memorable characters and touching musical score make it a standout Pixar movie that leaves a lasting impression on audiences of all ages.

Zindagi na milegi dobara

"Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara" is a captivating film that takes viewers on a scenic and emotional journey through Spain, exploring the deep bonds of friendship and the importance of living life to the fullest. Its stellar performances, breathtaking visuals, and thoughtful narrative make it a timeless Bollywood classic.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
